Instructions

1. On the start menu click the button PLAY.
2. You will be redirected and the game will start with a countdown of 3 seconds.
3. The game will display an image related to a JoJo's Bizarre Adventure Stand.
4. You need to guess the name of the Stand.
5. You will have 3 attempts and 5 seconds to introduce a name.
6. If you guess correctly, the image will be revealed and you will earn points and another image will be displayed.
7. If you guess incorrectly though, you will lose and will be asked if you want to play again.
